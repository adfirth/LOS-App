Competition Rules

1. Competition Name

The competition shall be known as "Altrincham FC Juniors National League 24/25 Last One Standing".

2. Entry Fee & Payment

  • The cost to play is Â£10 per game which secures the player 2 lives.
  • Payment must be made before the first fixture of the game.
  • Each game consists of 10 weeks of fixtures.
  • Payment can be made by cash or via the circulated payment link.

3. Participation

  • There shall be no maximum number of participants.
  • All participants must be aged 16 or over to play the game.
  • The Altrincham FC Juniors National League Last One Standing game is a fundraiser supporting Altrincham Junior Football Club so we will welcome and appreciate any external entries.

4. Game Objective

The game's objective is to pick a winning team in each of the 10-game weeks of Vanarama National League from the fixtures provided on the submission form available at https://bit.ly/AltrinchamJFCLastOneStanding.

5. Team Selection Rules

  • A player can only pick a team once in the game. i.e., the entry must have 10 different teams selected.
  • If the same team is selected on more than one occasion the pick will automatically move to the next unpicked team on their submission forms drop-down list (alphabetical).

6. Game Duration

  • The game shall run for a maximum of 10 game weeks, but a player could win before this if all other players have been eliminated.

7. Lives System

  • Once a player's selection draws or loses they will lose a life.
  • When 2 lives are lost the player is eliminated from the game.

8. Match Postponements

  • If a match is postponed or not played through a fixture change which occurs on the day the fixture is due to be played, both teams will be deemed as winners and no lives will be lost.
  • If a match is postponed or not played through a fixture change occurring before the day the fixture is due to be played, players should update their entry or the pick will automatically move to their next unpicked team on the submission forms drop-down list (alphabetical).

9. Prize Fund

  • The last player standing after 10 weeks or sooner will win the prize fund.
  • 50% of the money raised will go towards Altrincham Junior Football Club, the remaining 50% shall be the prize fund.
  • In the event of more than one winner at the end of the game the full prize fund will be shared equally with all winners.

10. Communication

  • Weekly updates will be provided by email and via the Altrincham JFC Last One Standing WhatsApp group.

11. Dispute Resolution

  • Altrincham Junior Football Club's Committee will have a final say in the unlikely event of any dispute.
